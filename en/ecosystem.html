FlatQube

EVER Wallet

EVER Wallet is a browser and mobile wallet available on IOS, Android and Google Chrome that allows you to conveniently and securely store and transfer your funds on the Everscale network from any device, wherever you are. EVER Wallet was developed by the Broxus team, one of the most promising and successful development teams on the Everscale network.

Octus Bridge

Octus Bridge is a platform developed by the Broxus team that enables the bi-directional transfer of tokens between the Everscale and other networks. The bridge allows users to move liquidity from Ethereum, Binance Smart Chain, Fantom and Polygon to the Everscale network, where transactions require minimal fees and take place at much higher speeds.
